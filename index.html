JoNews - In The Creation of Frankenstein is about a Robert Walton, an English adventurer, whom undertakes a expedition to the North Pole. While on his expedition his crew discovers a man who is near death from frostbite. The man who is near death is Victor Frankenstein The man tells a story about how he created a monster that went on to kill three of his most beloved relatives. The protagonist who was the true killer was not the monster, but Victory Frankenstein.
